Pizza recipe

Description

This is a pizza, it's round and it tastes really good, literally no better description

Ingredients

  • Dough: 3.5 to 4 cups all-purpose flour (or "00" flour),1.25 cups warm water,1 tsp active dry yeast,1 tsp sugar,2 tbsp olive oil,1 tsp salt
  • Sauce: 1 can crushed tomatoes,1 tsp salt,1 tsp dried oregano,1 tsp dried basil,
  • Toppings: 2 cups shredded low-moisture mozzarella,desired meats or vegetables

Steps

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a large bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until a foamy layer forms
  2. Make the Dough: Add the flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix and knead on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes until the dough is smooth and elastic
  3. First Rise: Place the dough in an oiled bowl, turn to coat, cover with a damp cloth or plastic wrap, and let rise for 1 to 1.5 hours until doubled in size
  4. Preheat and Shape: Preheat your oven to 475 F - 500 F (if using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up). Punch down the dough and stretch it out with your hands on a floured surface until it reaches a 10-12 inch circle
  5. Assemble: Transfer the dough to a dusted pizza pan or parchment paper. Spread a thin layer of sauce, followed by mozzarella and toppings. (Be careful not to overload it, or the crust will be soggy!)
  6. Bake: Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling
